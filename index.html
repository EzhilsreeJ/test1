About Saveetha Engineering College

Saveetha Engineering College (SEC), a premier institution of higher education in Chennai, India, was established with a vision to foster academic excellence and innovation. Affiliated with Anna University and accredited by NAAC 'A' Grade, SEC is committed to providing world-class technical education and research opportunities.

Our sprawling campus is equipped with state-of-the-art infrastructure, including modern laboratories, libraries, and student amenities, creating an ideal environment for learning and growth.

Departments

Computer Science and Engineering

Focuses on core computer science principles, algorithms, data structures, and emerging technologies like AI, ML, and Data Science.

Electronics and Communication Engineering

Covers VLSI design, embedded systems, signal processing, and telecommunications, preparing students for the dynamic electronics industry.

Mechanical Engineering

Offers a strong foundation in mechanical design, thermodynamics, fluid mechanics, and manufacturing processes, with specializations in robotics and automotive engineering.

Civil Engineering

Deals with infrastructure development, structural analysis, environmental engineering, and construction management, contributing to nation-building.

Electrical and Electronics Engineering

Explores power systems, control systems, instrumentation, and renewable energy sources, crucial for sustainable development.

Biomedical Engineering

Integrates engineering principles with medical and biological sciences to design and develop medical devices and healthcare solutions.
